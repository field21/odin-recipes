MAC AND CHEESEEEEE

This is the literal definition of mac and cheese, it cant get better than this

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Milk
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. In a large utensil,add some milk and turn the stove on.
  2. Now add some pasta and keep stirring the pasta at regular intervals.You can also add pepper and salt at this stage
  3. When the pasta has softened(boiled and edible) add in some cheese(a lot)
  4. Let the cheese dissolve, then stir it a little and VOILA
  5. Enjoy
