The best looking Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jam of your choice
  • Bread
  • Butter(optional)

STEPS

  1. Initially we begin by buttering one slice of our bread with peanut butter
  2. Next we jam the other slice of the bread
  3. Now we conjoin the two slices to make a perfect raw PB&J
  4. If you can go the extra mile, then switch on the stove,add some butter to the bread and toast it a little on both the sides
  5. You will melt!!!!
